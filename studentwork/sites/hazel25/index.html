TOP 6 BEST SHOWS

This is my opinion so these shows might not be the best for you :p

  1. Doctor Who

    Seasons: 41, Overall
    Genre: Science Fiction, Action, Drama
    Doctor Who is a great show. It's about a time lord who stole a tardis, which is a spaceship, and went to explore. He had to end a war between his people and the daleks leaving the doctor as the last time lord. The doctor gets different companions along the way and changes forms everytime he dies. This is a fun show with lots of amazing adventures. To watch there is a original series, a series in between, and a series on Disney. So far there have been 15 doctors and my personal favorite is David Tennant.

  2. Star Trek

    Seasons: 50?, over the course of many shows.
    Genre: Science Fiction, Action
    Star Trek is a great, it had many different shows, Next Generation, Voyager, Deep Space nine, Original series, and many more. It set in the future on starships that explore the universe. It is a great science fiction with many different adventures, and great, loveable characters. Basically every show has fun little "memories" from earlier series. Overall great shows.

  3. The Office

    Seasons: 9
    Genre: Sitcom, Comedy, Mokumentary
    The Office is a really entertaining mokumentary, it has great characters, fun episodes, and fun relationships between characters. The Office takes place in Dunder Mifflin, a paper company. It follows the characters in a documentary style and interviews them as well as showing what is going on. The office will have you laughing every episode.

  4. Avatar the Last Airbender

    Seasons: 3
    Genre: Adventure, Action, Fantasy, Comedy, Animation
    Avatar the Last Airbender is a great show. In this world there are benders of the four elements, air, water, fire, and earth. These benders control the elements that they are part of. For example if you were born in the earth kingdom, you may be a earth bender. However the avatar can control all four elements. But one hundred years ago the avatar disapeared, and the air temples have all been destroyed by the fire nation leader, leaving no air nomands left. One hundred years later Katara, a water bender, and her brother Sokka, a non bender, discover the new avatar named Aang, an airbender. Aang was alive a hundred years ago and he didn't realize his kind was destroyed. Although he was born over a hundred years ago he still looks twelve because he was trapped in a icebrg. Will Aang, Katara, Sokka, Aangs sky bison Appa, and friends they make along the way be able to defeat the fire lord? This is an amazing show with great charcters. Every episode is fun and it is definetly better than I described it! Must watch!

  5. Baking Impossible

    Seasons: 1
    Genre: game show, reality competition tv, food, engineering
    Baking Impossible is such a fun show. In this show there are multiple teams of one baker and one engineer. The teams make bakes that have to complete certain challenges and taste good. Overall, it is such a fun show and the teams are great.

  6. Nailed It

    Seasons: 7
    Genre: reality tv, game show, comedy/humorous reality show, food
    Nailed It is such a fun and funny show. The premise of nailed it is that they bring in amateur bakers. The inexperienced bakers have to recreate beautiful bakes. This show will always find a way to make you laugh. It is so funny and comforting! Must watch.
