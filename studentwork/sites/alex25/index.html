TOP 5 POKEMONS

This list includes in my opinion, the best pokemons with the best abilities. You can use this information in whatever way you see fit.

  1. Arceus

    Height: 10' 6"
    Category Alpha
    Energy Type: Colorless
    This pokemon, named Arceus is known far and wide for being a God pokemon, being the most powerful pokemon, creating the world, and the universe around it.

  2. Gengar

    Height: 4' 11"
    Category: Shadow
    Energy Type: Psychic
    This legendary pokemon is world-renowned for being a pokemon being able to enter in and out of the soul world freely. Gengar is also known for being a trickster. He finds it a hobby to decieve people.

  3. Pikachu

    Height: 1' 04"
    Category: Mouse
    Energy Type: Electric
    This OG Pokemon was one of the first 4 pokemon, being known for his signature electric tail. He is also known for his cuteness and his amazing ability to be turned into a soft, squishy plushie. He is one of the greats.

  4. Mewtwo

    Height: 6' 7"
    Category: Genetic
    Energy Type: Psychic
    This timeless legendary Pokemon is know far and wide for being the most powerful Pokemon in the game. He was created in a lab by messing around with the DNA of the most OG powerful Pokemon EVER. Mew. This is why he is named MewTWO. He is basically a better freak version of Mew. Mew is still pretty good though...

  5. Charizard

    Height: 5' 7"
    Category: Flame
    Energy Type: Fire
    This Pokemon is well known worldwide as an awesome Pokemon. Charizard is the face of most pokemon items alongside Pikachu. When a lot of people think of Pokemon, they often think of Pikachu first, then Charizard. Charizard is also known for holding the title of most powerful Pokemon, before Mewtwo came along. He is now one of the best OG pokemons ever. This guy is a legend.
